How do I get started?

如何开始使用？

Open the app, go to the Settings tab, choose an AI provider (Gemini, OpenAI, or Doubao), and enter your API key. Then create a journal for your trip and start adding bills.

打开应用，进入设置标签页，选择一个 AI 服务商（Gemini、OpenAI 或豆包），输入 API 密钥。然后创建一个旅行日记，开始添加账单。

How does AI bill recognition work?

AI 账单识别如何工作？

Take a photo of a receipt or select one from your library. The app sends the image to your chosen AI provider, which extracts the merchant name, amount, date, category, and line items. You can review and edit the results before saving.

拍摄收据照片或从相册选择。应用将图片发送到您选择的 AI 服务商，提取商户名称、金额、日期、类别和明细。保存前您可以检查和编辑识别结果。

What is the Minds feature?

什么是 Minds 功能？

Minds generates a sketch-style infographic timeline from your journal's bill data using AI image generation. It creates a visual summary of your trip spending.

Minds 功能使用 AI 图片生成，根据日记中的账单数据创建手绘风格的信息图时间线，直观展示您的旅行消费。

Where is my data stored?

数据存储在哪里？

All data — including journals, bills, receipt images, and settings — is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. AI requests go directly to the provider you choose.

所有数据（包括日记、账单、发票照片和设置）均存储在您的设备本地，不会上传到任何服务器。AI 请求直接发送至您选择的服务商。

Which currencies are supported?

支持哪些货币？

BillMind supports multiple currencies including CNY, USD, EUR, JPY, GBP, KRW, THB, and more. Each journal can have its own base currency.

BillMind 支持多种货币，包括人民币、美元、欧元、日元、英镑、韩元、泰铢等。每个旅行日记可以设置不同的基础货币。
