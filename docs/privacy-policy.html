1. Overview

一、概述

BillMind ("the App") is a travel bill tracking application with AI-powered invoice recognition. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

BillMind（以下简称"本应用"）是一款支持 AI 发票识别的旅行账单追踪应用。我们非常重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策说明了本应用如何收集、使用和保护您的信息。

2. Data Collection & Storage

二、数据收集与存储

Local Storage

All user data — including journals, bill records, receipt images, and settings — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not operate any servers and do not collect or upload your personal data.

Receipt Images

Photos of receipts and invoices are stored locally on your device. When you use AI recognition, images are sent directly to your chosen AI provider for processing. They are never sent to our servers.

API Keys

To use AI features, you provide your own third-party AI service API keys (e.g., Gemini, OpenAI, Doubao). These keys are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere other than the AI provider you selected.

本地存储

本应用的所有用户数据（包括旅行日记、账单记录、发票照片和设置）均存储在您的设备本地，使用 Apple SwiftData 框架。我们不运营任何服务器，不会收集或上传您的个人数据。

发票照片

收据和发票照片存储在您的设备本地。使用 AI 识别功能时，图片会直接发送至您选择的 AI 服务商进行处理，绝不会发送至我们的服务器。

API 密钥

为使用 AI 功能，您需要自行提供第三方 AI 服务的 API 密钥（如 Gemini、OpenAI、豆包）。这些密钥仅存储在您的设备本地，不会发送到除您选择的 AI 服务商以外的任何地方。

3. Third-Party Services

三、第三方服务

The App sends data to your chosen AI provider for processing, including:

本应用会将数据发送至您选择的 AI 服务商进行处理，包括：

  • Receipt/invoice image recognition and data extraction
  • Timeline infographic generation (Minds feature)
  • 收据/发票图片识别与数据提取
  • 时间线信息图生成（Minds 功能）

These requests are sent directly from your device to the provider — they do not pass through our servers. Please refer to each provider's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

这些请求直接从您的设备发送至对应服务商，不经过我们的服务器。请参阅各服务商的隐私政策了解其数据处理方式。

4. Device Permissions

四、设备权限

  • Camera — For photographing receipts and invoices
  • Photo Library — For selecting existing receipt images
  • Network Access — For sending requests to AI providers
  • 相机 — 用于拍摄收据和发票
  • 相册 — 用于选择已有的发票图片
  • 网络访问 — 用于向 AI 服务商发送请求

5. Data Sharing

五、数据分享

The App offers config import/export. In "Share" mode, API keys and sensitive credentials are automatically removed. In "Backup" mode, the file contains the full configuration. Please keep your backup files secure.

本应用提供配置导入/导出功能。在"分享"模式下，API 密钥和敏感凭据会被自动移除。在"备份"模式下，文件包含完整配置。请妥善保管您的备份文件。

6. Financial Data

六、财务数据

Bill amounts, categories, and other financial information are stored exclusively on your device. We have no access to your financial data. The App does not connect to any bank, payment processor, or financial institution.

账单金额、分类及其他财务信息仅存储在您的设备上。我们无法访问您的财务数据。本应用不连接任何银行、支付处理商或金融机构。
